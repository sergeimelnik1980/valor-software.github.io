Background

Coral is a science-backed app that helps couples and individuals improve their connections. Its engineers, who have been using the app for some time, know it better than anyone. With their exceptional knowledge and supervision, you won't be alone on your intimate journey. Coral gets to know you better and offers valuable insights from professors and practitioners.

We started working with Louis when his team already had a functional mobile app and web version. However, the current version has become outdated after a year since it was developed. Valor took on the task of code exploration, bug fixing, and eventually server reconsideration. This is where the real story begins :)

The mobile app using Cordova was created more than a year ago before we took it over. The web version uses Vue.js. The main challenge was with the admin system and its controls. We also worked on the web and mobile versions from time to time, and eventually released them on the App Store and Google Play Android Store.
