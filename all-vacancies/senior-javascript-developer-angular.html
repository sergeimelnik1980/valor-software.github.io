Senior JavaScript Developer (Angular)

Kharkov, Ukraine

Valor Software is looking for a JavaScript developer to join our company to work on an interesting project with customers from the USA. The project is originally known for leadership in enterprise resource planning (ERP) software. It's a market leader in end-to-end enterprise application software, database, analytics, intelligent technologies, and experience management. A top cloud company with 200 million users worldwide, it helps businesses of all sizes and in all industries to operate profitably, adapt continuously, and achieve their purpose.

As a part of our team, you will have the opportunity to build the next generation Angular framework for the client's existing and future products, to develop your skills with modern front-end frameworks, and fulfill your ideas in building production-ready solutions.

  • 5+ years of experience in software development

  • Experience in RxJS and NgRx
  • Strong HTML5, CSS3, SCSS knowledge
  • Unit-testing
  • Experience with Scrum/Kanban Agile frameworks
  • English (verbal and written): Intermediate and higher

  • Follow the development workflow
  • Write clean and well-structured code
